# Digital Marketing

## Overview

This repository showcases academic projects completed as part of the **Digital Marketing** course during the PGPM program at **Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon**.

The projects focused on applying digital marketing concepts to real-world business scenarios, including Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Social Media Marketing, E-commerce Optimization, Website Analytics, Competitor Benchmarking, and Performance Marketing.

The objective was to design data-driven marketing strategies by leveraging market research, consumer insights, keyword analysis, campaign optimization, and digital performance metrics.

---

## Course Objectives

- Understand Digital Marketing ecosystem
- Develop Google Ads campaign strategies
- Perform Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
- Analyze Social Media Marketing campaigns
- Optimize E-commerce product listings
- Evaluate website performance
- Understand consumer acquisition funnels
- Measure campaign effectiveness using KPIs

---

## Projects Included

### 1. Google Ads Campaign Strategy

Designed a complete Google Ads campaign for the Air Purifier industry by identifying customer segments, keyword opportunities, campaign structure, bidding strategy, and persuasive call-to-actions.

Topics Covered

- Market Research
- Customer Segmentation
- Keyword Research
- Search Intent
- Google Ads
- Campaign Planning
- Call-to-Action Design

---

### 2. Social Media Advertisement Analysis

Performed comparative analysis of Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns from leading air purifier brands including Philips, Dyson, Levoit, Blueair and Airth.

Topics Covered

- Meta Ads
- Audience Targeting
- Influencer Marketing
- Creative Strategy
- Campaign Messaging
- Consumer Engagement
- ROI Analysis

---

### 3. Website SEO & Domain Comparison

Compared Samsung and Apple websites using SEO metrics to evaluate digital visibility, organic search performance, domain authority, keyword rankings, backlinks, AI visibility, and traffic sources.

Topics Covered

- SEO
- Organic Search
- Paid Search
- Backlinks
- Domain Authority
- Keyword Analysis
- AI Visibility

---

### 4. Amazon Product Listing Optimization

Analyzed the Amazon product page of JBL PartyBox Club 120 and proposed recommendations to improve discoverability, CTR, customer engagement, and conversion rate.

Topics Covered

- Amazon SEO
- Product Listing Optimization
- Customer Reviews
- Pricing Analysis
- Conversion Optimization
- A+ Content
- Sponsored Ads
