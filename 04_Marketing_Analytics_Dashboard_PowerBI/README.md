# Marketing Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This repository showcases academic projects completed as part of the **Marketing Analytics** coursework during the PGPM program at **Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon**.

The projects focused on transforming marketing and customer data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards, KPI monitoring, customer segmentation, and data visualization using Power BI.

The objective was to enable data-driven marketing decisions by measuring campaign performance, customer behaviour, sales trends, and marketing effectiveness.

---

## Course Objectives

- Develop interactive marketing dashboards
- Analyze marketing performance using KPIs
- Measure campaign effectiveness
- Perform customer segmentation
- Visualize marketing trends
- Support business decision-making through analytics
- Automate marketing reporting

---

## Academic Projects

### 1. Marketing Performance Dashboard

Designed an executive dashboard to monitor overall marketing performance across multiple business dimensions.

Dashboard Highlights

- Campaign Performance
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Customer Acquisition
- Regional Performance
- Product Performance
- Revenue Analysis
- Executive KPI Dashboard

---

### 2. Customer Segmentation Analysis

Performed customer segmentation using marketing and transactional data to identify high-value customer groups and improve targeting strategies.

Activities Performed

- Customer Segmentation
- RFM Analysis
- Customer Profiling
- Purchase Behaviour Analysis
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Customer Retention Analysis

---

### 3. Campaign Performance Analytics

Analyzed digital marketing campaign effectiveness using key performance indicators to evaluate customer engagement and return on investment.

Metrics Included

- Click Through Rate (CTR)
- Cost Per Click (CPC)
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)
- Conversion Rate
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- Return on Investment (ROI)

---

### 4. Marketing KPI Dashboard

Developed Power BI dashboards for monitoring marketing KPIs and executive reporting.

KPIs Tracked

- Revenue
- Sales Growth
- Customer Growth
- Campaign ROI
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Conversion Funnel
