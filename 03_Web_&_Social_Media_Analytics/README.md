# Web & Social Media Analytics

## Overview

This repository contains academic projects completed as part of the **Web & Social Media Analytics** course during the PGPM program at **Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon**.

The projects focused on evaluating website performance, measuring social media effectiveness, analyzing customer engagement, and deriving actionable marketing insights using web analytics, SEO metrics, and social media KPIs.

The coursework emphasized data-driven decision-making by measuring campaign performance, understanding user behaviour, benchmarking competitors, and optimizing digital marketing strategies.

---

## Course Objectives

- Understand Web Analytics fundamentals
- Measure website performance using analytics metrics
- Analyze social media engagement and audience behaviour
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness using KPIs
- Perform SEO and competitor benchmarking
- Interpret customer acquisition and conversion metrics
- Develop actionable digital marketing recommendations

---

## Academic Projects

### 1. Website Analytics & SEO Performance Analysis

Conducted a comparative analysis of Samsung and Apple websites to evaluate their digital presence using SEO and website performance metrics.

Key Activities

- Website Traffic Analysis
- Organic vs Paid Traffic Analysis
- Domain Authority Comparison
- AI Visibility Assessment
- Backlink Analysis
- Keyword Ranking Evaluation
- Competitor Benchmarking

---

### 2. Social Media Campaign Analytics

Analyzed Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns from leading consumer brands to understand campaign performance, audience targeting, engagement strategies, and content effectiveness.

Brands Analyzed

- Philips
- Dyson
- Levoit
- Blueair
- Airth

Activities Performed

- Campaign Performance Evaluation
- CTA Analysis
- Creative Content Analysis
- Audience Targeting
- Influencer Marketing Assessment
- Engagement Strategy Comparison

---

### 3. Social Media Metrics Dashboard

Studied key social media metrics used to evaluate campaign success across digital platforms.

Metrics Covered

- Reach
- Impressions
- Engagement Rate
- Amplification Rate
- Virality Rate
- Audience Growth Rate
- Social Referrals
- Return on Investment (ROI)

---

### 4. Consumer Engagement Analysis

Evaluated customer interactions across digital platforms by measuring engagement, referrals, conversions, and audience growth trends.

Focus Areas

- Customer Journey
- Audience Behaviour
- Referral Traffic
- Content Performance
- Campaign Optimization
