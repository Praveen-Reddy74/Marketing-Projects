# Marketing Management II

## Overview

This repository contains academic projects completed as part of the **Marketing Management II** course during the PGPM program at **Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon**.

The course focused on advanced marketing concepts including brand management, product strategy, pricing, integrated marketing communications (IMC), customer relationship management (CRM), channel management, and strategic marketing decision-making.

Through industry case studies and marketing frameworks, the course emphasized creating sustainable competitive advantage by developing customer-centric marketing strategies and evaluating business growth opportunities.

---

## Course Objectives

- Develop strategic marketing plans
- Understand brand management and positioning
- Design customer-centric marketing strategies
- Analyze pricing and distribution decisions
- Evaluate Integrated Marketing Communication (IMC) campaigns
- Study Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Apply marketing frameworks to real-world business scenarios
