# Spatial Computing in Marketing

## Overview

This repository contains academic projects completed as part of the **Spatial Computing** course during the PGPM program at **Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon**.

The course explored how Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Digital Twins, and Location Intelligence are transforming customer experiences, retail environments, and digital marketing strategies.

The projects focused on understanding how immersive technologies enable organizations to create personalized customer journeys, enhance brand engagement, optimize retail operations, and improve business decision-making.

---

## Course Objectives

- Understand the fundamentals of Spatial Computing
- Explore Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Mixed Reality (MR)
- Analyze real-world business applications of immersive technologies
- Evaluate customer engagement using spatial technologies
- Understand Digital Twin concepts
- Study location-based marketing strategies
- Examine AI and IoT integration within spatial environments
