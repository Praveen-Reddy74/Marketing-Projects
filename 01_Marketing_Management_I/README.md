# Marketing Management I

## Overview

This repository contains the academic work completed as part of the **Marketing Management I** course during the PGPM program at **Great Lakes Institute of Management, Gurgaon**.

The course focused on understanding customer needs, designing competitive marketing strategies, and developing data-driven marketing decisions through real-world business case studies.

---

## Course Objectives

The primary objectives of this course were to:

- Understand core marketing concepts and frameworks.
- Analyze customer behavior and buying decisions.
- Develop market segmentation, targeting, and positioning (STP) strategies.
- Evaluate competitive landscapes and industry attractiveness.
- Design effective marketing mix (4Ps) strategies.
- Apply strategic marketing concepts to business case studies.
